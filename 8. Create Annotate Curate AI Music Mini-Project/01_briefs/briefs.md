## BRANO A — Instrumental

Title: Glass Echoes in Motion

Genre: Modern neo-classical minimalism (contemporary cinematic underscore)

Duration (target): 1:30 – 1:45

Tempo: ~90 BPM (steady pulse)

Key: A minor

Mood: contemplative, minimalist, hypnotic, emotionally restrained

Function: underscore / background emotional cue

Instrumentation:
- felt piano (ostinato-based)
- soft muted strings (con sordino)
- subtle reversed textures
- warm ambient pads
- light percussive ticks (textural only)

Texture & Energy:
- low to medium density
- gradual layering
- energy curve: 2 → 5 / 10

Target Structure:
- Intro: sparse piano gestures
- Pulse I: piano ostinato + subtle strings
- Pulse II: harmonic thickening + pad bloom
- Release: thinning textures, emotional closure

Constraints:
- no heavy percussion
- no dramatic cinematic hits
- no EDM or rhythmic drive
- no abrupt endings

Target Use Case:
AI music evaluation dataset

Title: Stay in the Blue Light

Genre: Future R&B / Alternative Pop

Duration (target): 2:40 – 3:00

Tempo: 78 BPM (steady, laid-back groove)

Mood: intimate, moody, futuristic, sensual

Function: song cue / modern pop-R&B listening track

Vocal:
- female lead vocal
- soft, breathy delivery
- intimate and close-miked
- emotionally restrained, non-belting

Instrumentation:
- warm Rhodes electric piano
- clean sub-bass (sine-like, steady)
- airy sustained pads (background)
- light trap-lite drum kit (kick, snare, subtle hi-hats)
- soft synth plucks (rhythmic accents)

Texture & Energy:
- medium density
- groove-driven, not busy
- energy curve: 3 → 6 / 10
- focus on vocal clarity and space

Target Structure:
- Intro
- Verse 1
- Pre-Chorus 1
- Chorus 1
- Verse 2
- Pre-Chorus 2
- Chorus 2
- Bridge
- Chorus 3
- Outro

Constraints:
- no aggressive EDM risers
- no rock-style drums
- no excessive distortion
- no overpowering reverb on vocals

Target Use Case:
AI music training dataset for vocal QA
